Quality of an Iteration
* Measure how well a cycle was executed.
* Focuses on : compilation of tasks, meeting requirements, low defects, efficiency , and applying feedback.
	

Quality of Outcome Determination
* Measure how good the final result is .
* Focuses on : correctness, reliability , stakeholder satisfaction, standards, compliance, and measurable performance
	





Sprint definition




A fixed ,time-boxed agile iteration to complete a set of tasks and deliver a usable product increment.












	 


Determination of length of sprint.


Usually 1-4 weeks, chosen based on team size, project complexity,feedback needs, and delivery frequency .

Ideal day / Ideal hour 


   * Focus on the task; No interruptions
   * Constantly able to focus; No interruptions
	Problems with Ideal estimation


* Doesnt  account for interruption,meeting, or distraction
* Often overly optimistic compared to real-world conditions.
* Can lead to missed deadlines if used alone for planning.
	



Daily Standup
Purpose
A short daily meeting to synchronize the team, track progress and identify blockers.
	3 Questions


* What did i do yesterday ?
* What will i do today ? 
* Any blockers or obstacles?
	Rules


* Keep it short 
* Focus on progress and issues, not problem solving 
* Everyone stands up to stay concise.
* No deep discussions.
